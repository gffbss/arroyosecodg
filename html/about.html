Principal

Founder and Principal Steve Boss has been working in commercial real estate for nearly three decades. He graduated in 1986 from The University of Southern California with a BA in Econ and Finance. The knowledge, experience and expertise Steve has developed over time led to the founding of ArroyoSeco Development Group with several institutional-grade equity partners. .

Since its founding, ArroyoSeco Development Group has been a highly respected boutique real estate investment company with a strong history of success. We have completed over $60 million in development and re-development projects throughout our target market.

About ArroyoSeco Development Group

ArroyoSeco Development Group was founded in 2000 by 15 year industry veteran Steve Boss along with several strong private equity investment partners. Over the years, the company has developed and redeveloped numerous retail shopping centers, multi-family and office properties throughout the western United States.

ArroyoSeco Development Group has a proven reputation for integrity in relationships with investors, brokers, service providers and stakeholders. We value our relationships and have worked with team members on many projects throughout our company history.

Our Philosophy

The guiding philosophy at ArroyoSeco Development Group is to create strong investment returns relative to the market while creating lasting relationships. We are proud of the projects we have built and renovated for the communities we have worked in.
