Lawndale, CA.

Arroyo Seco Development Group, in conjunction with the City of Lawndale, plan to develop a new retail/restaurant destination shopping center at the former redevelopment site at the south west corner of Hawthorne Blvd. & Manhattan Beach Blvd.

Project Vision

Create an attractive shopping center that promotes convenience, economic vitality, entertainment and a pleasant quality of life. The project will provide access to a range of tenants and will incorporate public gathering spaces.

Design & Tenant Mix

The development will be designed to incorporate basic features like easy access, abundant and available parking. Also, specific features like outside seating, open space for kids and families and contemporary architecture will be part of this new development.

The selected tenants will be designed to appeal to current, modern tastes of millennials. The tenant mix will encourage people to stay longer, to enjoy their City and to relax.
