Paso Robles, CA

Afton secured control of a 12 acre development site on Highway 46 at Golden Hill in Paso Robles which was strategically located and ripe for near-term development of a retail shopping center. Through our marketing efforts Lowe's Home Improvement showed interest in the site. There was also interest from additional potential tenants that we wanted to try to accommodate, so the adjacent property owner was contacted and we were able to interest them in contributing their property to a larger master development plan. The project became large enough that we engaged an institutional development partner and negotiated a joint venture development of the site with them. The site is now a thriving shopping center.
